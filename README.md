Chaos & Dabbers CIC is a digital community supporting adults experiencing loneliness, neurodivergence, caregiving stress and social isolation. Through interactive online events, peer support groups and skill-based quiz games, we create accessible spaces for connection and wellbeing.
NEED:
Rising loneliness
Carer burnout
Lack of accessible community spaces online
Neurodivergent social exclusion
WHAT WE DO:
Weekly themed quiz & bingo-style skill games
Peer support groups
Carer support sessions
Body doubling sessions for ADHD adults
Creative community engagement
OUTCOMES:
Reduced isolation
Improved wellbeing
Increased social connection
Peer support networks
Accessible community participation
